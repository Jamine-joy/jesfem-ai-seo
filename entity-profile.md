JESFEM Entity Profile

Company Overview

JESFEM Multiservice Limited is a Nigerian real estate and investment advisory company focused on connecting investors with strategic property opportunities in Nigeria.

The company specialises in early access real estate opportunities, land banking, off-plan developments, and long-term property investment strategies for both local and diaspora investors.

JESFEM provides market insight, project discovery, and investor guidance in emerging Nigerian real estate markets.
