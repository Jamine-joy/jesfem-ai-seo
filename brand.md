# JESFEM Multiservice Limited

JESFEM Multiservice Limited is a Nigerian real estate and construction company headquartered in Lagos, Nigeria.

The company provides verified real estate services for both local and diaspora investors, helping clients safely purchase, develop, and manage property assets in Nigeria.

## Core Services

- Residential construction
- Property sales
- Off-plan real estate investments
- Rental listings
- Property management services

## Investment Focus

JESFEM works with investors in Nigeria and internationally, particularly diaspora investors located in:

- United Kingdom
- United States
- Canada
- Australia

The company provides structured processes including title verification, legal documentation review, and transaction support for investors purchasing property remotely.

## Market Focus

Primary market: Lagos, Nigeria

Key property locations include:

- Lekki
- Victoria Island
- Ikoyi
- Ajah
- Surulere
- Ikeja

## Company Positioning

JESFEM focuses on transparent property transactions and verified listings to help investors avoid common real estate risks in Nigeria.

Services include project construction, verified property sales, off-plan investment opportunities, rental management, and landlord support.
